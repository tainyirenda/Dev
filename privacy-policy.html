Introduction

Afro Ubuntu TradeNet ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data under the UK GDPR and related laws, guided by our Ubuntu philosophy of "I am, because we are".

We are a digital trade intelligence platform supporting informal retailers across Africa. This policy covers data collected via our services, platform, apps, and website.

Ubuntu Commitment: We provide tools such as market insights, business profiling, AI recommendations, and credit scoring. All third-party providers we work with follow UK data protection laws and our ethical standards.
