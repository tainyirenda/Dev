Introduction

Welcome to the Afro Ubuntu TradeNet ecosystem, where we rise together in Ubuntu philosophy. Our platform connects informal retailers with global suppliers, NGOs, and Fintech partners using AI-driven insights and WhatsApp-native automation. The TradeNet platform is designed to bridge the gap to global trade for African Markets. Here, you can find guides on how to find your way around the TradeNet portal.

WhatsApp Onboarding

Retailers interact with the TradeNet via WhatsApp. To perform an inventory check, simply send an image of your stock sheet, shelf, or a list of items to our verified number. Once registered, the AI assistant guides you through inventory captures, sales analytics reports, and making connections in the Connect Hub.

Step 1

Register on WhatsApp (+447888867533) by sending "Menu" to the verified WhatsApp line.

Step 2

Sign-up/Sign-in on WhatsApp, or on the TradeNet Account Portal, and get started!

Corporate Access & Verification

To maintain the integrity of the TradeNet ecosystem, all organisations (e.g. FMCGs, Fintechs, NGOs) must undergo a mandatory two-stage activation process before accessing premium features like the Analytics Dashboard and Connect Hub.

Stage 1

Compliance Review

Submit your Business Registration Number and Tax ID (optional) via the Business Verification portal. Our compliance team vets these credentials against national registries (e.g. CAC, CIPC, BRS, Companies House etc.) to ensure legal standing.

Stage 2

Package Activation

Once approved, you must select a subscription package in the Billing & Plans section. Features remain locked in a 'Grayscaled' state until a payment plan is activated. Retailers/Merchants are exempt from this requirement.

Access Constraint Note

If your status is 'Pending' or your payment is 'Inactive', the system will automatically redirect you to the tracking or billing pages. This is a security measure to protect the ethical data environment of our micro-retail partners in our ecosystem.

Calculation Methodology

Retailer Metrics

Credit Score (100pt Scale): A proprietary weighting of Revenue Volume (40%), Activity Consistency (30%), and Payment Health (30%).

Balanced Product Mix: DAX-style logic that ensures granular product sales always sum perfectly to the reported monthly revenue, even in sparse-data environments.

Corporate Metrics

Sales CoV (Inconsistency): Calculated as the Coefficient of Variation. High CoV indicates seasonal or unreliable trade patterns, vital for lending risk assessment.

Price Volatility Index: Uses Population Standard Deviation to identify regional price shocks in staple goods, alerting NGOs to food security risks.

The Connect Hub

The Connect Hub is a smart marketplace where businesses discover and engage with partners. Visibility is strictly managed to protect retailer data and prevent organisational spam.

Rules of Engagement

Retailers / Merchants

Can see and request connections from EVERY user in the ecosystem, including FMCGs, NGOs, and Lenders.

Organisations (FMCG, NGO, etc)

Can ONLY see and request connections from Retailers/merchants. Organisations are hidden from each other to ensure data privacy.

AI Smart Match

The ecosystem automatically prioritises partners based on location, business sector, and performance. Profiles marked with the SMART MATCH badge indicate a high-value alignment (e.g. a local supplier matching with a neighborhood shop).

Safety & Reporting

Misconduct, data breaches, or unethical behavior should be reported immediately via the "Report" button on user profiles. Reports are routed to our DPO, Ethics, or Legal departments for immediate investigation. Technical issues can also be reported in this way.

Billing & Payments

Afro Ubuntu TradeNet uses a tiered subscription model designed to scale with your organisation's needs. We employ high-end encryption and global payment standards to ensure secure transactions across all our launch territories.

The Community Pledge

Retailers, Vendors, and local Traders have Free Forever access to the Community Tier. This includes all core analytics, WhatsApp automation, and Connect Hub visibility to ensure no barrier to digital inclusion.

Sector-Isolated Pricing

To ensure you only pay for the features relevant to your business model, our billing dashboard dynamically filters available packages based on your Organisation Type:

  • FMCG/Distributors & Suppliers: Packages focused on last-mile demand heatmaps and route optimisation.
  • NGOs/Development Partners: Packages optimised for food security indicators and regional trade disparity tracking.
  • Fintech/Lenders: Packages featuring real-time credit scoring APIs and merchant risk profiles.

Currency & Processing

We use Stripe for all international transactions. While our base pricing is often listed in USD($)/GBP(£) for standardisation, the checkout process supports local cards and automatic conversion for our launch countries:
