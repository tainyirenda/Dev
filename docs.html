Introduction

The Afro Ubuntu TradeNet is a unified digital ecosystem designed to bridge the gap between informal micro-retailers and formal financial and supply chain sectors.

WhatsApp Onboarding

Retailers start by registering their WhatsApp-linked phone number in the portal. Once registered, the AI assistant guides them through inventory captures using simple photo uploads.

Step 1

Register phone number in the Account Portal.

Step 2

Send "Menu" to the verified WhatsApp line.

Merchant Guides

Merchants can access their real-time performance via the "Free Community Tier." This includes sales trends, stock alerts, and an AI-generated credit profile.

Note: Always ensure that inventory photos are taken in good lighting to ensure the AI Worker can accurately parse SKU counts and pricing.

API Integration

For Fintech and Bank partners, the following endpoint provides access to the normalized credit tiers of connected retailers.

GET Request

GET /api/v1/analytics/credit-score?target_id={business_id}
Authorization: Bearer YOUR_API_TOKEN
