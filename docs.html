Merchant Guides

Retailers participate in the ecosystem via our WhatsApp-native interface. To get started:

  1. Register your phone number via the Web Portal.
  2. Send "Hi" to our verified WhatsApp number.
  3. Upload a photo of your recent inventory or sales log.
Tip: Ensure images are clear and well-lit for optimal AI recognition.

API Integration

Fintech partners can pull real-time credit metrics using our authenticated endpoints.

Request Example

GET /api/v1/analytics/credit-score?target_id={business_id}
Authorization: Bearer YOUR_API_TOKEN
