Violations & Incident Response

Report AI Ethics Concerns

If you believe our AI has made an unfair decision, violated ethical principles, or caused harm, we want to hear from you immediately. Every report is taken seriously.

How to Report
  • • Email: privacy@afroubuntutrade.co.uk
  • • In-App: Report button on every AI interaction
  • • Anonymous: Anonymous reporting option available
What to Include
  • • AI decision or recommendation details
  • • Why you believe it was unfair/biased
  • • Impact on you or your community
  • • Any supporting evidence or documentation
