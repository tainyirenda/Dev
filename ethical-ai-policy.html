Ethical AI & Data Use Policy

AI that empowers, respects, and builds trust

Version 1.0 — July 2025

Purpose of This Policy

Afro Ubuntu TradeNet is committed to building AI systems that empower informal retailers, respect user rights, and build trust across African trade ecosystems.

This policy outlines how we apply ethical principles in the development and deployment of our AI-powered trade systems, including computer vision, NLP, mobile data analysis, and credit scoring.

Core Principles

Transparency

We inform users when AI is used and explain decisions clearly. No black boxes - our AI recommendations come with explanations you can understand.

Consent & Data Sovereignty

All data collection is opt-in. Retailers own their data and control how it's used. We believe in data sovereignty - your data, your choice.

Non-Discrimination

We audit our models to prevent bias based on language, literacy, image quality, or location. Every trader deserves fair treatment regardless of their circumstances.

Purpose Limitation

Data is used only for stated purposes. Any sharing requires explicit consent. We don't repurpose your data without your knowledge and agreement.

Human Oversight

All AI decisions can be appealed. Critical decisions involve human review. Technology serves people, not the other way around.
