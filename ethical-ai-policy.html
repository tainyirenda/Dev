Afro Ubuntu TradeNet is committed to building AI systems that empower informal retailers, respect user rights, and build trust across African trade ecosystems.

1. Purpose of This Policy

This policy outlines how we apply ethical principles in the development and deployment of our AI-powered trade systems, including computer vision, NLP, mobile data, and credit scoring.

2. Core Principles

  • Transparency: We inform users when AI is used and explain decisions clearly.
  • Consent & Data Sovereignty: All data is opt-in. Retailers own their data.
  • Non-Discrimination: We audit models to prevent bias in language, literacy, or image quality.
  • Purpose Limitation: Data is used only for stated purposes, with consent for sharing.
  • Human Oversight: All AI decisions can be appealed. Critical decisions involve human review.

3. Data We Collect

  • Retailer name, location, and business profile
  • Product images, messages, and pricing data
  • Behavioral signals like upload frequency and stock patterns

4. AI Use Cases & Model Auditing

All models are tested for fairness and accuracy. We maintain a model logbook tracking training datasets, performance by region/gender, and updates.

5. Violations & Reporting

If you believe your data was misused or AI made an unfair decision, contact us at: info@afroubuntutrade.co.uk
